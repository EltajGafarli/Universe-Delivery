Who We Are?

Universe Delivery LLC Company is an online delivery company. Deliveries are on foot. Univesr Delivery LLC Company delivers home and office supplies from major department stores and pharmacies. Orders will be placed through the Universe Delivery application and the call center.

Services

There are 2 main services.
1) Online delivery: then you choose the products you want in the application and they are delivered to your home as soon as possible (max time limit is set at 40 minutes)
2) Delivery assistance. When you have difficulty transporting the product you bought from the store to your home, you can call one of the employees of Universe Delivery LLC Company for help by selecting the help section in the application. (The time limit for the employee to reach for help is set at max 5 minutes)

About Us

It is a company that does online delivery. Our distinctive feature is that deliveries are only on foot. It is very easy to register as an employee or customer at Universe Delivery LLC Company. All you need to register as a customer is a phone number and email address. To register as an employee, all you need is the FIN code, phone number and email address of the identity document. An identity document is important to ensure that the employee is over 18 years of age. In addition to delivery, the company will also provide a delivery service. Universe Delivery LLC Company offers this service to its customers with everyone in mind.
